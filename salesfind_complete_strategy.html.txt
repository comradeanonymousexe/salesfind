                <h4>Content Mix:</h4>

            <h2>Platform-Specific Best Practices</h2>

            <h3>LinkedIn Algorithm Optimization</h3>
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Comment before you post:</strong> Engage for 15 minutes before publishing</li>
                <li><strong>First hour is critical:</strong> Respond to every comment within 5 minutes</li>
                <li><strong>Use 3-5 relevant hashtags:</strong> #B2BSales #LeadGeneration #SalesStrategy</li>
                <li><strong>Native content wins:</strong> No external links in main post (add in first comment)</li>
                <li><strong>Tag strategically:</strong> Mention 1-2 relevant creators (not overused)</li>
            </ul>

            <h3>X/Twitter Algorithm Optimization</h3>
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Threads > single tweets:</strong> 1 thread per week minimum</li>
                <li><strong>Reply-guy strategy:</strong> Add value to conversations from larger accounts</li>
                <li><strong>Quote tweet with insight:</strong> Don't just retweet, add perspective</li>
                <li><strong>Use trending formats:</strong> Adapt popular meme templates to B2B context</li>
                <li><strong>Pin your best tweet:</strong> Update weekly with top performer</li>
            </ul>

            <h3>Instagram Algorithm Optimization</h3>
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Reels get 3x reach:</strong> Prioritize short-form video</li>
                <li><strong>First 3 seconds matter:</strong> Hook must stop the scroll</li>
                <li><strong>Save rate is key:</strong> Create reference-worthy content people bookmark</li>
                <li><strong>Stories build intimacy:</strong> Daily stories drive feed engagement</li>
                <li><strong>Use mix of hashtags:</strong> 15-20 hashtags (5 high-comp, 10 mid-comp, 5 niche)</li>
            </ul>

                <li><strong>Headline:</strong> Bold/Extra Bold, 48-72pt (stop the scroll)</li>
                <li><strong>Subheadline:</strong> Medium/Semi-Bold, 24-36pt (expand on headline)</li>
                <li><strong>Body Text:</strong> Regular, 16-20pt (deliver value)</li>
                <li><strong>Caption:</strong> Light/Regular, 12-14pt (credits, fine print)</li>
            </ul>

            <h3>The "Scroll-Stopper" Checklist</h3>
            <ul class="checklist">
                <li>Can headline be read from 6 feet away?</li>
                <li>Is there high contrast between text and background?</li>
                <li>Does the design have a clear focal point?</li>
                <li>Is there negative space (not cluttered)?</li>
                <li>Does color palette align with brand?</li>
                <li>Is text readable on mobile screen?</li>
                <li>Would I personally stop scrolling for this?</li>
            </ul>

            <h2>Community Building Strategies</h2>

            <h3>The "Community Architect" Method</h3>

            <div class="highlight-box">
                <p><strong>Tier 1: Super Fans (10-20 people)</strong></p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Most engaged followers who comment on every post</li>
                    <li>Strategy: DM personally, ask for input, feature in posts, offer early access</li>
                </ul>

                <p><strong>Tier 2: Active Engagers (50-100 people)</strong></p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Regularly like/comment, occasionally share</li>
                    <li>Strategy: Respond thoughtfully, remember names, reference past conversations</li>
                </ul>

                <p><strong>Tier 3: Lurkers (Rest of audience)</strong></p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Follow but don't engage much, consume silently</li>
                    <li>Strategy: Create content that converts lurkers to engagers with polls, questions</li>
                </ul>
            </div>

            <h3>The "Engagement Spiral" Technique</h3>
            <ol>
                <li>Someone comments on your post</li>
                <li>You respond with thoughtful reply + question</li>
                <li>They respond again (2nd engagement)</li>
                <li>You check their profile and comment on their recent post</li>
                <li>They notice, follows you, engages more</li>
                <li>You DM thanking them for engagement</li>
                <li>Relationship formed</li>
            </ol>
            <p><strong>Result:</strong> Algorithm sees sustained engagement, boosts both of your content</p>
