Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our services, pricing, and process.

What does SalesFind do exactly?

SalesFind is a B2B lead generation and sales enablement agency. We help businesses identify high-quality prospects, qualify leads with data-driven methods, and deliver them directly to your sales team — so you can focus on closing deals instead of chasing cold contacts.

How do you generate leads?

We use a multi-channel strategy that combines targeted email outreach, LinkedIn prospecting, data enrichment, and AI-powered lead scoring. All leads are carefully researched and verified to ensure they match your ideal customer profile (ICP) before delivery.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients start seeing qualified leads within the first 2–4 weeks of launching a campaign. Full results and consistent lead flow typically build over 1–2 months as targeting and messaging are refined based on data insights.

What industries do you work with?

We work with B2B companies across multiple industries — including SaaS, IT services, marketing, manufacturing, and professional services. Our data-driven approach allows us to adapt to any niche where quality leads and pipeline growth are essential.

Can you integrate with our CRM or sales tools?

Yes! We can deliver leads directly into your CRM (like HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive) or connect with your existing sales stack via API and automation tools. This ensures a seamless handoff between lead generation and your sales process.

Is there a minimum contract or commitment?

We offer monthly subscription plans, but we recommend a minimum 3-month engagement to allow sufficient time for data gathering, testing, and optimization. This ensures you get maximum ROI from our lead generation strategy.
